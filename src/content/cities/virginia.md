## Welcome to Virginia, MN

Virginia sits at the heart of the Iron Range, surrounded by lakes and forests that belie its industrial heritage. The city offers affordable living, strong community bonds, and easy access to the outdoor recreation that defines northern Minnesota life.

## Real Estate Value

Virginia is one of the most affordable real estate markets in Minnesota. Buyers will find solid family homes well under $150,000, with lakefront and rural properties available at prices that simply don't exist in southern Minnesota.

## Outdoor Access

Virginia is surrounded by the Laurentian Divide, dozens of lakes, and direct access to the Superior Hiking Trail. The nearby Minntac and Hibbing Taconite operations provide economic stability to the broader region.
