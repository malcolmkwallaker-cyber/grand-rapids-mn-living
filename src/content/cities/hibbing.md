## Welcome to Hibbing, MN

Hibbing is the largest city on Minnesota's Iron Range and one of the most affordable places to buy a home in the entire state. Known as the birthplace of Bob Dylan and Greyhound Bus, Hibbing blends working-class pride with a surprisingly rich cultural scene and outdoor access.

## Affordable Real Estate

Hibbing offers some of the most affordable home prices in Minnesota. Single-family homes are available from under $100,000, with well-maintained family homes typically in the $120,000–$175,000 range. For buyers priced out of other markets, Hibbing offers tremendous value.

## The Iron Range Heritage

The Hull-Rust-Mahoning Open Pit Mine — one of the largest open pit iron ore mines in the world — runs right along the north edge of the city. The Hibbing Taconite plant remains one of the region's largest employers, providing economic stability.
