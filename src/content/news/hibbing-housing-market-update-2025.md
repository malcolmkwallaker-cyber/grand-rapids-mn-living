Hibbing, Minnesota continues to rank among the most affordable housing markets in the state, according to 2025 data from the Minnesota Realtors Association. The Iron Range city's median home price remains well below both the state median and the national median, creating ongoing opportunities for first-time buyers and investors.

The data shows median home prices in Hibbing holding in the $130,000–$140,000 range — compared to a statewide median approaching $340,000. For buyers priced out of Twin Cities suburbs or looking to maximize purchasing power, Hibbing represents a compelling alternative.

Local factors contributing to affordability include a relatively stable supply of mid-century housing stock, lower-than-average property taxes compared to metro markets, and economic stability provided by ongoing Iron Range mining operations.

**Bridger Hopkins, REALTOR® at Pemberton Real Estate** notes that buyer interest from the Twin Cities has increased noticeably in recent months, with out-of-area buyers increasingly recognizing the value proposition that Hibbing offers. "We're seeing buyers who can work remotely realizing they can own a significantly larger, higher-quality home in Hibbing for what they'd pay for a small condo in the metro," Hopkins said.
