## Hibbing Housing Demand Holds Steady as Iron Range Fundamentals Stay Strong

Hibbing continues to draw attention from buyers looking for affordability that no longer exists in most of Minnesota. Recent data suggests the median home price in Hibbing is sitting around $135,000, which puts it among the most accessible markets in the state for buyers who want actual ownership rather than another decade of renting. According to local reports, listings in the $90,000 to $180,000 range are moving at a consistent pace, with modest single-family homes near Greenhaven and the Brooklyn neighborhood generating the most interest.

The economic backdrop remains a big part of the story. Hibbing Taconite, operated by Cleveland-Cliffs, continues to anchor employment in the area, and industry updates over the past year suggest ongoing operations at the plant with no signs of the disruption seen at some other Iron Range facilities. Fairview Range Medical Center is another stable employer, and city records indicate ongoing investment in healthcare infrastructure to serve the broader northern St. Louis County population. Between mining, healthcare, and education through Hibbing Community College, the local job base is more diversified than outsiders assume.

On the community side, the Hull-Rust-Mahoning Mine View continues to bring tourism dollars into town, and recent reporting on visitor traffic suggests the site remains one of the top draws in the region. The Greyhound Bus Museum and the ongoing Bob Dylan tourism connection also give Hibbing a cultural identity most Iron Range cities don't have. Local reports indicate steady interest in downtown storefronts along Howard Street, which has seen a slow but real revitalization over the past several years.

Infrastructure updates have been more incremental than dramatic. City records indicate routine street and utility work through the summer season, and school district planning discussions have continued around long-term facility needs at Hibbing High School, the historic building funded by mining wealth over a century ago and still one of the most architecturally significant public schools in the country.

For real estate, the takeaway is simple. Hibbing offers a rare combination in Minnesota: sub-$150,000 entry points, employment stability, and a community with real identity. Out-of-state buyers and investors have been quietly finding this market for a few years, and inventory that would have sat six months ago is now moving faster than it did in 2022 or 2023.
