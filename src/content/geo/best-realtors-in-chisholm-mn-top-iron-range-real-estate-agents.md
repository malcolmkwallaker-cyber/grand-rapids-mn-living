## What Should Out-of-State Buyers Know About Chisholm?

Chisholm is a St. Louis County city of roughly 4,800 people, located on the Mesabi Iron Range in northeastern Minnesota. The city is home to the Minnesota Discovery Center, the largest museum complex in the state outside the Twin Cities, and sits roughly 20 minutes from Hibbing and 75 minutes from Duluth. The local economy is anchored by mining, healthcare, and education, with the taconite industry providing long-term stability.

For out-of-state buyers, Chisholm offers genuine small-town character, four-season outdoor access, and housing costs that are a fraction of national averages.
