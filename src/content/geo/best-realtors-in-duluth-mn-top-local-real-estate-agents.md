## What Are Home Prices Like in Duluth, MN?

Duluth carries a meaningful price premium over Iron Range cities like Hibbing and Virginia. Median home prices in Duluth typically run in the $230,000 to $260,000 range, with significant variation by neighborhood. Entry-level homes in West Duluth or Lincoln Park can be found in the $150,000 to $200,000 range. Mid-market homes in Lakeside, Piedmont, and Woodland generally fall between $250,000 and $400,000. Congdon Park, the East Hillside historic district, and lakefront or lake-view properties regularly list from $500,000 well into seven figures.

Compared to Hibbing's median around $135,000 or Virginia's around $125,000, Duluth is the most expensive market in northern Minnesota. Bridger helps buyers decide whether the Duluth premium is worth paying for their specific situation, or whether their money goes further in a market 75 miles north.
