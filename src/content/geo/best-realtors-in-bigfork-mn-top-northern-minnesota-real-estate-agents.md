## What Is Bigfork, MN Like as a Place to Live?

Bigfork is a small town in northern Itasca County, roughly 40 miles north of Grand Rapids on Highway 38, the Edge of the Wilderness Scenic Byway. The community is small, the character is genuinely rural, and outdoor access is the main draw. Scenic State Park sits just north of town. The Big Fork River runs through the area and is a working river for paddlers, anglers, and cabin owners. Bigfork has a hospital, a school, a grocery store, and the basic services you need, but it is not a place people move to for shopping and restaurants. It is a place people move to for space, quiet, and access to the outdoors.
