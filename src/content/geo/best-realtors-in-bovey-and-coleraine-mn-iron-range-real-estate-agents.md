## What Are Home Prices Like in Bovey, MN?

Bovey is one of the more affordable markets in Itasca County. Typical single-family homes trade between $75,000 and $180,000. The housing stock is largely early-to-mid 20th century construction from the mining boom era, meaning solid bones, older systems, and often smaller lots than what you would find in Grand Rapids or Cohasset. Property taxes in Bovey are low relative to most of Minnesota, which is one reason retirees and investors keep buying here.
