## Where Is Keewatin, MN, and What Is the Local Market Like?

Keewatin is located in northeastern Itasca County, roughly 6 miles west of Hibbing and about 30 miles east of Grand Rapids along U.S. Highway 169. The town sits directly in the Mesabi Iron Range mining corridor, and its economy is tied to the same taconite and mining employment that stabilizes Hibbing and the surrounding Range communities.

The local market is quiet, steady, and dominated by owner-occupants and long-time residents. Turnover is lower than in tourist-heavy lake markets. When homes do come up, they often sell quickly to buyers who understand the value. Bridger tracks Keewatin listings closely because inventory can be thin and timing matters.
