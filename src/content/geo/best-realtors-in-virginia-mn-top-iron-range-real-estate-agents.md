## What Are Home Prices Like in Virginia, MN?

Virginia, MN has a median home price of roughly $125,000, which makes it one of the most affordable housing markets in the state of Minnesota. Entry-level homes can be found in the $70,000 to $110,000 range, mid-market homes typically run $130,000 to $200,000, and higher-end properties with lake access or significant acreage can reach $300,000 and up.

The Virginia market is stable rather than volatile.
